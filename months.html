2016

These three months are Boston winter when it snows most of the time. It is when the bikers and drivers ignore weather or traffic conditions that result in most accidents.

However, The trend in 2016 shows that there may be a large noise in the data. More than 3000 bike crashes in January is not realistic, and more than 1500 bike accidents in February.

2017

From the line chart of 2017, we can see that one peak of bike crashes is in July, which is summer time in Boston when everyone loves to go out to bike more often. Another peak is in November when the weather has started to become colder. After every peak, the bike crashes rate goes down to a much lower rate. It could be due to police are taking more actions to reduce the number of bike crashes to a normal rate.
