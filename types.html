Lack of facilities or need improvements

836 accidents due to lack of facilities or need improvements, which makes up of 9% of all crashes

What should we do?

The reason of the largest proportion of crashes remains unknown from this dataset, for further research, we can conduct interviews, surveys, observations, or divide the question into smaller categories to understand the underlying safety hazard. Yet, we could see from this dataset that we should raise people’s awareness of speeding. People are educated about speeding, but they need to be constantly reminded that accidents could happen to everyone if they’re not careful. Another improvement is proposed by a Harvard study to record better police reports on bike crashes. Lusk and her colleagues discovered that most reports are handwritten and drawn by police, and only few details about the bikes are shown on diagrams. To better understand and reduce bike crashes, we’ll need more bicycle-specific codes or diagrams.
