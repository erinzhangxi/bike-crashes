Correlational Statistics - Month & Number of Crashes

H1: Months of the year and number of bike crashes are significantly correlated.

H0: There’s no significant correlation between the months and number of bike crashes.

2016 data

r(10) = -0.808, p < 0.05

There’s a significant negative correlation between months of the year and number of bike crashes.

2017 data

r(10) = 0.2156371, n.s.

We're unable to conclude that there's a significant correlation between months in 2017 and number of crashes.
