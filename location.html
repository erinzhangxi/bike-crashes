Descriptive Statistics - Locations

Bike Crashes Heatmap in 2017

There are usually some traffic crashes hotspots in the city. In 2017, most crashes took place in the following areas:

Massachusetts Avenue from Back Bay toward Charles River

Commonwealth Avenue next to Storrow Drive

Intersections on Boylston Street

Columbus avenue

Park Street MBTA station

Among the crashes hotspots, however, there are some common features that can help us understand what locations could potentially lead to more accidents. Crashes usually take place in main street, where cars maintain at a higher speed.
